    <a target="_blank" data-toggle="collapse" data-target=".collapse-two" class="collapse-header text-center definition">
      <h2>Instructions</h2>
    </a>
    <div class="collapse collapse-two collapse-body text-left">      
      <div class="briefing border">
        <img alt="Crowdflower logo." src="http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/e/e1/Crowdflower-logo.png" style="width:100px;float:right;margin:15px;">
        <div>
          <p>
            You will be given a task which will require you to ask for information about a <i>connection from one place to another</i>, about the <i>number of transfers</i>, <i>next connection</i>, <i>time of departure</i>, <i>weather across the US</i>, <i>what is the time</i>, etc.
          </p>
          <p>You can say <i>"Help."</i> for hints how to use the service. If it seems confused, you can say <i>"Reset."</i> or <i>"New entry."</i> to start over. The service can pick up what you are saying and ask you questions as the dialogue goes along.
          </p>
          <ul>
            <li>Make sure you are in a quiet place, turn off disturbing elements such as TV, for the system may pick up other sources of noise and therefore misbehave.</li>
            <li>Call a US toll free number <strong>1-888-826-5115</strong> and you will be connected with the automated Public Transport Information service.</li>
            <li>After you are connected, please try to talk naturaly about the topic presented in the green box at the bottom.</li>            
            <li>Talk in whole sentences, don't say just key words.</li>
            <li>The call is finished by saying <span style="color: red;">"Thank you. Good bye."</span> to the system.</li>
            <li>At the end of the call, the system will give you a <strong>four digit code</strong> which you have to enter into a field down below, once the call is terminated. This is used to verify the authenticity of Job submissions.</li>
            <li>You will not be given any code if you do not exchange at least three sentences with the system, so do not rush the dialogue towards the end too quickly. Just chat a litte.</li>
            <li>When correct code is entered, it will allow you to fill in a short feedback form.</li>
            <li>You are more than wellcome to enter general comments and observations.</li>
          </ul>
        </div>
        <p>The feedback is very important as only the calls with the correct code and completed feedback will be accepted.</p>
